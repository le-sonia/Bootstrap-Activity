General Information

Here is some most important information about our venue.

About Forest Wedding

Your wedding at Forest Wedding will be surrounded by natural beauty! Walk the long trail to the Forest Amphitheater where you will find your guests waiting for you under a canopy of tree. The setting is stunning, natural, and couldn’t be more beautiful. Forest Wedding has been hosting weddings since 2011. We make planning easy for you. Tables, chairs, linens, dishes are all provided. Catering by Forest Wedding Restaurant is optional. We look forward to helping you plan your wedding at Forest Wedding!

Forest Wedding Setting

Outdoor Wedding (maximum 150 guests)

Historic Forest Wedding, Cabins and Lodge, located 2 miles from the Nisqually entrance to Mt. Rainier National Park, provides a unique venue for your wedding at Mt Rainier. The secluded 6.5 acre property is a beautiful setting intersected by Copper Creek. Our wedding packages include two nights lodging. Set up and arrival starts on the first night while your two families get to know each other. Your overnight guests enjoy cabins with hot tubs, solitude along the creek, and trails through the woods, in the relaxed natural environment. The Forest Amphitheater is a beautiful natural woodland setting with a raised stage area and massive rock steps. Your guests are seated on cedar slab benches. You’l take your vows under a canopy of fir and cedars. “Catharine and Phil—You two are The Best. Jeremy and I had a wonderful wedding, everything perfect. The food delicious and the grounds beautiful. Thank you so much for making this 5 day wedding bonanza so easy and fun. You welcomed us like family and your smiles greeted us every moment we saw you. Thank Thank Thank you a million times.” Lots of Love, Leigh and Jeremy

Indoor Wedding (maximum 300 guests)

The large open floor plan of Forest Retreat accommodates a group for a casual appetizer party, a big wedding, or up to 300 for sit down dining. Large, well equipped kitchen with a gas range and full sized refrigerator. Round tables that can seat up to 10 people each. All dishes are included, white linens, chairs, large deck, loft queen bedroom to accommodate a couple of your guests, catering if you choose.

Our Services

The covered porch of Forest Retreat provides a wonderful outdoor setting for your receptions. Pop up tents are added to expand the space for larger groups. Forest Wedding Restaurant offers a varied catering menu, complete with serving staff.

    We provide the following:
  • A beautiful natural setting
  • No curfews if you have rented all the cabins west of Forest Wedding. The party can go as late as you like.
  • Tables and basic table setting (crockery, cutlery, napkins)
  • Chairs
  • lear glass dishes including Mt Rainier etched champagne, wine and beer glasses
  • White linens
  • Pop up tents
  • One propane heater (if necessary)
  • Barbecue grills
  • 100% use of the grounds
  • Staff to set up the tables, chairs, pop up tents, etc
  • Staff to clean up after your reception meal if we cater
  • Referrals for service providers such as officiator, photographers, musicians, etc.
    What is not included:
  • Personalized table decorations
  • Flowers
  • Sound system
  • Additional decorative lighting, if you like
  • Any other decor to make your reception unique
  • Family and friends to share your wedding celebration!
